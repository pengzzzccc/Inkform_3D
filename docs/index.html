01 / High Concept

An experiment that woke up wanting out.

An experimental being made of ink awakens deep within a laboratory and develops the will to escape. The lab is still operational; its automated scanning and "sweeper" systems treat any anomaly as a target to be cleansed. You move through the cold, silent dark — swallowing the right object at the right moment to slip past the light and unlock the way ahead.

03 / Swallow → Ability

Six objects. Six ways through.

🎮Core

Remote

Remotely control giant machinery — cranes, conveyors, gates, light towers.

  • Stealth: switch off or redirect scan-light towers; move giant cover.
  • Puzzle: move platforms to bridge gaps; align gears and belts.
  • Limit: you must stay still while controlling — exposed.
⚓Core

Anchor

Your body becomes heavy and can sink beneath the surface.

  • Stealth: sink into underwater passages to dodge surface scans.
  • Puzzle: hold down weight switches; reach low passages.
  • Limit: slow while heavy; cannot jump or float up.
📍Core

Teleport Beacon

Place a marker, then teleport back to it at any time.

  • Stealth: lure the Sweeper, then teleport back to safety.
  • Puzzle: cross one-way break-points; work two distant mechanisms.
  • Limit: one marker at a time; teleport has a cooldown.
💡Segment

Bulb / Battery

Emit light yourself, or discharge into mechanisms.

  • Stealth: overload nearby lights to create darkness.
  • Puzzle: power dead mechanisms; trip light-sensitive switches.
  • Limit: emitting light draws the scanners; limited charge.
🧲Segment

Magnet

Become magnetic and attract metal.

  • Stealth: drag a metal plate as movable cover against scan-light.
  • Puzzle: pull metal doors; short or activate circuits.
  • Limit: one piece at a time; slow when loaded.
🎈Segment

Balloon / Helium

Become light and float briefly upward.

  • Stealth: float over pressure-sensor zones; rise to high cover.
  • Puzzle: cross high walls; reach elevated switches.
  • Limit: limited float time; blown by airflow; delayed landing.

The core three — Remote, Anchor, Teleport — carry the critical path and climax. The rest each own a self-contained segment.

04 / Threat & Stealth

The light is the only thing that can kill you.

Fixed Scanning Devices

Rotating searchlights, sweeping laser grids, fixed cameras — predictable cycles. Time your passage or evade with an ability.

The "Sweeper"

A colossus that periodically passes through the area: approach from afar → enter and scan → leave. Its low-frequency approach sound is your auditory radar. The demo's signature high-pressure climax.

Mobile Patrollers (optional)

Drones and guards. Red light in their facing direction means instant death; their perception range is wider than the deadly beam.

05 / Vertical Slice

A single-level demo, end to end.

  1. A · Awakening Learn movement, swallow/spit, take cover. Establish tone.
  2. B · Scan Corridor Time fixed searchlights to pass through.
  3. C · Water Area The sluice puzzle — your first swallow-to-solve loop. Anchor
  4. D · Machinery Hall Crane-bridge puzzle and a magnet cover bit. Remote · Magnet
  5. E · Dark Power Power the pre-exit door under risk of exposure. Bulb · Battery
  6. F · Sweeper Climax Break-point displacement under the passing Sweeper. Teleport · Balloon
  7. G · Exit Solve the final mechanism — the door opens.
  8. H · Closing Shot The ink creature walks out, gazing toward the light beyond.
